The Creation

PokéBot was the first pokemon capturing discord bot that allowed the creations of other bots such as Pokecord and such to be created. This bot was number one, until it was shut down back in late 2017. Three years later, the project has been revived for users to enjoy once again



How do I Start

Its very simple, just start with p.pokemon and see what you obtain, then try your luck in gambling by first starting off with p.daily. To view the many other commands availible, just do p.help and you will see!



Can I trade shinies?

Yes, if you have a shiny pokemon and want to trade it, you can do so! All you need to do is just p.trade @User , THEN when it asks you what pokemon you are trading you can put the shiny pokemon name
